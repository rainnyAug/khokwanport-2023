Bobtle

    Bobtle is a computer game that created in Game 2D class, and it was created in Unity.
Rules - Players will receive 3 hearts and have to collect all the bottles as specified. If the player hits an enemy, the hearts will be reduced. When the hearts run out, the game is over. After collecting all the bottles, players have to walk to the portal to go to the next level.

Story
In a world that has monsters that can live with humans. Bob is a monster who works as a cleaner. His responsibility is to collect all of the bottles in every area. This world also has monsters that are unfriendly to both humans and monsters called “monterup”. They resisted and hindered the missions of friendly monsters.
